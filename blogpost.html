The Perfect: Coachella-Inspired Weekend

Whether you're actually headed to the Coachella Valley or chasing the vibes locally, it's easy to get inspired by the free-spirited, laid-back feeling that we've come to stylishly collect under the blanket term of "festival fashion." Capture it with playful-yet-practical pieces that get the whole open-fairgrounds je ne sais quoi thing—no wristband required.

The Bikini Top

Looking like you're always ready for a chic pool party is part and parcel of keeping Coachella-cool. Wearing a bikini as you would a crop top is also key for allowing maximum airflow.

The Shirt

A breezy white button-down can be styled in a ton of different ways: tuck, button, or knot it (or leave it open to fly freely in the breeze).

The Earrings

Bold, playful earrings are the surefire way to take an outfit to the next level. Since tassels are quickly becoming ubiquitous, get ahead of the style curve with bunched gumdrops instead.

The Shorts

Denim cutoffs are a summer, and festival, mainstay. Refresh the look by picking a patched pair with some personality.

The Camera

Mobile phones mean everyone's a photographer, but go a step further and actually print your sun-soaked memories out on command.

The Shoe

Mules are easy to kick off, yet still provide some of the coverage of a bootie (a practical option for dusty, dirty spaces). Skip the trendy white hue for an outdoors-friendly snakeskin print.

The Sunscreen

Sun damage isn't fun; spraying with SPF actually kind of is. Stock up before heading into the desert (or wherever your destination may be).

The Fanny Pack

In the age of overstuffed totes and shoulder bags, going hands-free is always a pleasure. It makes even more sense if you plan to be out and about.

The Sunglasses

Sunglasses have to be the wardrobe workhorse all too often. For Coachella, or any other fun-focused event, pick a pair that's outside the box, be it via shade or shape.

The Hotel

Beyond Coachella weekends, Palm Springs and its surrounding zip codes make for a nice breather from real life.
